Top 5 DC Comic Book Titles

1. The Dark Knight Returns

This iconic graphic novel by Frank Miller tells the story of an older Batman who comes out of retirement to fight crime in a dystopian future Gotham City. It's celebrated for its dark tone and mature themes.

2. Watchmen

Written by Alan Moore and illustrated by Dave Gibbons, Watchmen is a deconstruction of the superhero genre. It explores the lives of retired superheroes in an alternate history and is considered one of the greatest graphic novels ever.

3. Swamp Thing

Created by Len Wein and Bernie Wrightson, Swamp Thing follows the story of Alec Holland, a scientist who transforms into a humanoid plant creature. The series is known for its horror elements and complex storytelling.

4. Sandman

Written by Neil Gaiman and illustrated by various artists, Sandman tells the story of Dream, one of the Endless, who is captured and must escape to regain his realm. The series blends mythology, horror, and fantasy.

5. Kingdom Come

Written by Mark Waid and illustrated by Alex Ross, Kingdom Come is a story set in a future where superheroes have become violent and out of control. It explores themes of responsibility and redemption.
